 SERAPHINE NOCTVAULT
 concept: 
"Original action-RPG heroine. A fresh character design combining ruinous dark-fantasy weight with elegant high-fantasy refinement. Not based on any existing character."
  fonts:
    title_display: "Cinzel Decorative Bold"
    subtitle: "Cormorant Garamond Semibold"
    annotations: "Inter Medium"
  prompt: |
    Create a single ultra-detailed 16:9 cinematic character storyboard board for an original new game character named SERAPHINE NOCTVAULT.
    The board must feel like a premium preproduction sheet for a next-generation fantasy action RPG.
    The design language is a fresh fusion of oppressive gothic ruin fantasy and luminous aristocratic crystal fantasy.
    Keep the character completely original and newly designed.
    Do not imitate any existing franchise character, costume, weapon, or emblem.

    BOARD FORMAT:
    One-page horizontal production board, dark charcoal background, thin antique gold divider lines, highly organized grid layout, elegant typography, dense but readable annotations.
    Use beautiful fonts: main title in Cinzel Decorative Bold, subtitles in Cormorant Garamond Semibold, technical notes in Inter Medium.
    The title SERAPHINE NOCTVAULT should appear at the top in large refined capitals, with a smaller subtitle beneath:
    "THE GLASS SAINT OF THE ASHEN BASILICA"

    CHARACTER IDENTITY:
    Adult female knight-mystic, late 20s, tall and statuesque, severe grace, pale olive skin with faint silver undertone.
    Face: elongated oval, sharp cheekbones, calm eyes, solemn expression, slightly parted lips, long neck.
    Hair: long black hair with muted silver strands, partially braided, partially loose, wind-frayed.
    Silhouette: cathedral-slim upper body, layered armor skirt, long trailing cloth panels, asymmetrical shoulder mass.

    COSTUME DESIGN:
    Armor is original: blackened steel, smoked silver trim, translucent crystal insets, aged ivory enamel, charcoal velvet underlayers.
    Breastplate has vertical chapel-like fluting, not sexualized.
    One shoulder guard is larger and wing-like, the other narrower for asymmetry.
    Gauntlets are articulated and elegant, with glass-vein inlay.
    Waist drapery: layered tattered silk panels over chain and leather belts.
    Boots: heeled sabatons with cracked crystal spurs.
    Materials should feel expensive, ancient, ceremonial, and battle-worn.

    WEAPON:
    Primary weapon is an original longsword called "Lumen Dirge".
    Blade: elongated, narrow, faint internal glow as though a shard of frozen moonlight lives inside.
    Crossguard: cathedral-arch geometry, subtle thorn motifs.
    Pommel: faceted crystal reliquary.
    Include separate callout panels for sword details and a broken scabbard.

    BOARD SECTIONS:
    1. Header strip with project metadata:
       genre: dark fantasy action RPG
       board type: character key art + turnaround + combat beat guide
       palette: obsidian, ash gold, smoked silver, moonlit cyan, faded ivory
       style notes: sacred decay, tragic nobility, restrained ornament, premium game concept art

    2. Character reference section:
       full-body front, side, back, and three-quarter turnaround
       helmetless portrait close-up
       hand detail, armor material detail, sword detail, crystal relic detail
       small silhouette variants exploring cape spread and stance

    3. Key illustration:
       large hero keyframe of Seraphine standing in a ruined basilica nave under fractured stained-glass light, ash drifting through cold air, sword tip resting on cracked stone, faint cyan light reflecting off crystal armor seams.

    4. Story beat panel strip, 6 panels:
       Panel 1: wide shot entering the basilica transept
       Panel 2: close-up of hand gripping the luminous sword hilt
       Panel 3: low-angle stance as crystal halo fragments activate behind her
       Panel 4: sword slash releasing a narrow arc of glass-like light
       Panel 5: kneeling moment beside a fallen relic knight
       Panel 6: final solemn portrait, eyes lifted toward a broken rose window

    5. Notes area:
       Include lighting notes, combat posture notes, fabric movement notes, and emotional keywords:
       "votive", "severe", "elegiac", "disciplined", "hollow sanctity"

    ENVIRONMENT CUES:
    Ruined basilica architecture, collapsed columns, candle racks, broken saint statues, ash haze, fractured stained glass, wet stone floor catching light.

    RENDERING GOAL:
    Premium AAA concept art, richly detailed, highly legible, cinematic, elegant, haunting, original.
    Typography should be beautiful and integrated into the layout, not random floating text.
  negative_prompt: |
    no existing franchise characters, no copied armor, no logo clutter, no messy collage, no cheap poster look, no exaggerated fan-service anatomy, no low detail, no modern sci-fi weapons, no cartoon proportions, no unreadable annotations, no duplicate poses
