# I see Blender 3D to Seedance V2 going viral these days. It reminds me of the end

**Source:** https://x.com/IamEmily2050/status/2070691999162777656  
**Date:** Sat Jun 27 02:13:38 +0000 2026  
**Category:** Video Prompts

---

I see Blender 3D to Seedance V2 going viral these days. It reminds me of the end of January and February, when early testers got excited about these ideas, with references like 3D and audio driving performance. Still, the models weren't good enough to use Blender over MCP. Until recently, with GPT 5.5 and Opus 4.8, we started to see some progress, but we are still early. I believe it will take another six months to realise the full potential. I want to remind people that you can actually do full production work with Blender, not just a broken 3D demo, and with Unreal Engine 5.8 MCP, the future of the prompt will be totally different. We might generate assets in Unreal Engine, then create 480P videos with Seedance V2.5, and then reconstruct them in Blender. However, for sure, we will see a mix of the three in different ways, existing future, and we are really still early in all of this.
Let's keep exploring the possibilities, hopefully we also see a ComfyUI node link all of them together.
