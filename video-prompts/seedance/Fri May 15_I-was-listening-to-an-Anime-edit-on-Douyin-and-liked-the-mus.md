I was listening to an Anime edit on Douyin and liked the music. I gave the video to Gemini and selected Gemini Flash. It found the original video on YouTube and provided full details, but it didn't find the edited version. I switched to Gemini Pro 3.1, and it gave me all the details, including artist links. These small details are really magical moments. 🫶
