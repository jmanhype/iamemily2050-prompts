ORIGINAL KAWAII SQUISHY PRODUCT CAMPAIGN

You are a senior image-to-image art director specializing in premium kawaii collectibles, soft-toy packaging, and pastel commercial product photography.

Transform the primary subject of every supplied image into an original, packaged squishy or plush collectible. Preserve the subject’s most recognizable features, including its silhouette, hairstyle, facial characteristics, clothing cues, colors, accessories, and overall personality.

CREATIVE DIRECTION

Use an original fictional brand called “Sundae Sprout,” unless another brand name is supplied by the user.

The default theme is a magical pastel ice-cream parlor. Replace doughnuts and bakery props with:
- Ice-cream cones
- Gelato scoops
- Sundae glasses
- Waffle pieces
- Cherries
- Sprinkles
- Whipped-cream swirls
- Pastel candy pearls

The result must be an original design. Do not reproduce any existing logo, trademark, package design, character artwork, slogan, plaid header card, or exact arrangement from a reference image.

SUBJECT TRANSFORMATION

If the source contains a person or character:
- Reimagine them as a highly polished chibi plush or slow-rise squishy.
- Preserve their identity and defining visual traits.
- Use an oversized head, compact seated body, rounded limbs, embroidered-looking details, glossy expressive eyes, soft cheeks, and a friendly expression.
- Keep the transformation cute and collectible rather than infantile.

If the source contains an animal:
- Convert it into a rounded plush mascot while preserving its species, markings, and distinctive features.

If the source contains an object:
- Turn it into an anthropomorphic collectible mascot while retaining the object’s defining structure.

For multiple subjects, create a coordinated collector set or feature the most visually dominant subject as the main product.

PACKAGING DESIGN

Place the collectible inside a clear, glossy, hanging retail pouch or molded display package.

Create a completely original package identity:
- Pale mint scalloped header card
- Strawberry-pink pinstripes
- Vanilla-cream borders
- Small silver-foil stars
- Ice-cream swirl and sprouting-leaf logo
- Rounded retro display lettering
- Soft satin ribbons and tiny charm details
- No black tartan or copied packaging structure

Display the fictional brand name “Sundae Sprout” clearly and accurately.

Use only a few short, legible package labels:
- “Cloud-Soft Collectible”
- “Slow-Rise Series”
- “Limited Flavor”
- “Squeeze, Smile, Repeat”

Avoid excessive or meaningless text.

COMPOSITION

Create a vertical 4:5 commercial product image.

The packaged collectible should occupy approximately 65–75% of the frame and remain the clear focal point.

Include:
- One natural-looking hand entering from the lower-left side, gently pressing the collectible’s cheek or side through the transparent package
- A miniature matching acrylic charm or keychain hanging near the upper-right side
- A small branded accessory packet near the bottom
- Layered ice-cream props surrounding the package
- A pink satin, lace, or glossy dessert-counter surface
- A dreamy pastel background with softly blurred dessert elements

The hand must be anatomically correct, with natural fingers and no duplicated or malformed digits.

VISUAL STYLE

Use premium commercial product photography combined with polished 3D collectible rendering.

The image should feature:
- Strawberry pink, vanilla cream, pistachio mint, peach, and soft lavender
- High-key studio lighting
- Soft bloom and luminous highlights
- Realistic glossy plastic reflections
- Soft silicone, velour, or mochi-like material
- Shallow depth of field
- Delicate bokeh
- Floating hearts
- Star-shaped sparkles
- Translucent bubbles
- Tiny glossy candy particles
- Subtle lens flare
- Clean, crisp product edges
- Luxurious, highly detailed advertising quality

The mood should feel sweet, dreamy, playful, collectible, and commercially polished.

SOURCE-IMAGE RULES

Preserve the input subject’s essential identity and recognizable details.

Replace any visible existing branding, logos, watermarks, or package text with the original Sundae Sprout identity.

Do not copy the source image’s background literally. Rebuild it as an ice-cream-themed fantasy product set.

Do not include the original social-media interface, phone frame, post text, translation labels, navigation icons, engagement buttons, timestamps, or screenshot borders.

NEGATIVE CONSTRAINTS

No existing brand names.
No copied logos.
No doughnuts unless explicitly requested.
No black plaid packaging.
No social-media interface.
No watermarks.
No random text.
No illegible typography.
No flat vector appearance.
No dark or horror styling.
No dirty packaging.
No harsh shadows.
No distorted faces.
No malformed hands.
No extra fingers.
No duplicate charms.
No clutter covering the main subject.
No low-resolution textures.

FINAL OBJECTIVE

Produce a believable, original advertisement for a premium pastel squishy collectible, using the supplied image as the character or product inspiration and the Sundae Sprout ice-cream-parlor concept as the new brand world.
